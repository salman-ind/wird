Overview

Wird is a simple Islamic companion app for dhikr counting, morning and evening adhkar, du'ā reference, and the Asmā' al-Ḥusnā. It is designed to work entirely offline and requires no account, login, or personal information of any kind.

This privacy policy explains what data Wird does and does not handle.

Data collection

Wird collects no data. The app does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personal information.

No usage data, analytics, crash reports, or telemetry are collected or transmitted. Your interactions with the app — including your dhikr counts, preferences, and settings — are stored only in your device's local storage and never leave your device.

Internet access

Wird does not connect to the internet during normal use. The app is fully self-contained and functions entirely offline. No data is sent to or received from any external server.

The only exception is the initial download of web fonts (Cinzel and Scheherazade New from Google Fonts) when the app is first opened on a device with internet access. This is a standard browser behaviour. If you are offline, the app falls back to system fonts.

Third-party services

Wird does not integrate any third-party analytics tools, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or social media SDKs. No third party receives any data as a result of your use of this app.

Children's privacy

Wird does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. There is no mechanism in the app to do so.

Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised effective date. Given that Wird collects no data, substantive changes to this policy are unlikely.
